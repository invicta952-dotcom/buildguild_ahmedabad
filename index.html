Build Guild is a 1-day hardware meetup, part of Hack Club Blueprint's global Build Guilds week (April 13–19). It's 100% free, open to teens, and for anyone who wants to build hardware, attend workshops, demo projects, and meet their community.
Completely. Hack Club funds per attendee — covering the venue, food, and materials. You pay nothing, ever.
Not at all. Build Guild is for complete beginners and experienced makers alike. We have beginner-friendly workshops and open build time for those with existing projects.
Anyone aged 13–18. You don't need to be a Hack Club member — just a curious teen who wants to build things.
We'll share a parental consent form closer to the event. All attendees need signed permission from a parent or guardian.
Bring a laptop (Optional) and your phone. If you own hardware tools — soldering irons, multimeters, components, or a project you're working on — bring those too! We'll have soldering irons and basic tools available at the venue, but having your own gear means more build time for you.
